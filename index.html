Kaiwen Zhou

Hi there! I am a researcher at the Chinese University of Hong Kong.

I was a research scientist at Huawei Noah's Ark Lab from 2022 to 2025. Prior to that, I completed my MPhil and PhD in computer science and engineering at the Chinese University of Hong Kong in 2019 and 2022, respectively. I obtained my BSc degree in computer science and technology at Fudan University in 2017.

I am currently interested in research topics related to Embodied AI, Agent and Optimization for Machine Learning.
